Bitcoin MACD Calculator

Background

The MACD is a tool used by quantitative analysts to gauge market trends. It involves comparing the differential between a two-week and month-long moving average. Whether or not the MACD can actually predict future movement is debated, but the fact that some analysts use it often makes it a self-fulfilling prophecy. If enough investors base their decisions on the oscillation of the MACD, their collective behavior can influence the underlying.
